Civil War Rules

(Fast Paced Beer Pong)

*DRINK BEFORE SHOOT RULE APPLIES*

  • Three cups for each player â€“ the cups are considered as lifelines.
  • Can shoot at any time.
  • One ping pong ball per player.
  • Once you get a hold of a ball you can toss it towards any of your opponents glasses.
  • Once a teammate shoots a ball into the opponents cup that opponent has to drink the cup and loses a life. While that person is drinking, the other players are still allowed to continue.
  • If two balls are tossed in the same glass in succession, that player is automatically eliminated and has to drink all his or her remaining cups.
  • If two balls are popped into two different cups on the same row, the player has to drink from both cups and return the balls to their respective shooters.
  • Passing a ball to one of your teammates is allowed.
  • Players cannot lean or rest their elbows on the table while shooting.
  • Bounce shots do not count.
